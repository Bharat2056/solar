Welcome to J.B.N Surya Urja

Pioneering Renewable Energy Solutions in Nepal.

Founded in 2022 and registered in Tarkeshwar, Kathmandu, Nepal, we are a renewable energy company dedicated to solar power generation. We specialize in developing efficient, reliable, and scalable energy solutions that support Nepal’s transition toward sustainable power.

With a steadily expanding project portfolio, we are strengthening our operations and contributing to the nation’s evolving energy landscape.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in advancing renewable energy in Nepal through sustainable, reliable, and forward-looking solar power solutions.

Our Mission

To develop and deliver sustainable solar energy solutions that ensure reliable power generation, expand clean energy infrastructure, and contribute to Nepal’s long-term energy security.
