Welcome to J.B.N Surya Urja

Founded in 2022 and registered in Tarkeshwar, Kathmandu, Nepal, we are a renewable energy company dedicated to solar power generation. We specialize in developing efficient, reliable, and scalable energy solutions that support Nepal’s transition toward sustainable power.

With a steadily expanding project portfolio, we are strengthening our operations and contributing to the nation’s evolving energy landscape. Our facilities reduce structural dependencies on traditional power networks while providing clean, highly efficient assets for regional communities.
