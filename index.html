System Knowledge Base

Common inquiries regarding deployment and financing.

Is smart solar integration affordable upfront?
Yes. We offer $0-down capital financing models alongside the current 30% Federal Tax Credit. This allows most clients to achieve immediate net-positive cash flow, swapping a volatile utility bill for a lower, fixed hardware payment.
How invasive is the roof deployment process?
Our hardware mounts are designed to be non-invasive and actually protect the underlying roofing material from UV and weather degradation. Every deployment includes a comprehensive structural warranty.
What is the required maintenance protocol?
Modern solid-state solar hardware has zero moving parts, requiring near-zero maintenance. The companion app continuously monitors individual panel performance, instantly alerting our team if efficiency drops.
